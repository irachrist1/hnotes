### Navigation Pattern Research

**Modern apps (Airbnb, Uber, Apple Health) avoid:**
- Traditional top AppBars (dated, wastes screen space)
- Poor contrast (blue AppBar on blue gradient)
- Heavy headers

**What they use:**

1. **Bottom Navigation** (3-5 main sections)
   - Persistent, accessible
   - Icons + minimal labels
   - Examples: Uber, Instagram

2. **Gesture-Based** (swipe between screens)
   - No visible nav elements
   - Examples: Apple Health, Photos

3. **Floating Action Button (FAB)**
   - Primary action (+ to log meal)
   - Bottom-right or center
   - Examples: Gmail, MyFitnessPal

4. **Full-Screen Immersive**
   - No AppBar, status bar blends
   - Back button floats when needed
   - Examples: Airbnb, Instagram stories

**For HNotes:**
- Bottom Nav: Dashboard | Log | Stats | Profile
- FAB for quick meal logging
- No AppBar (full gradient)
- Swipe gestures
